This research project focuses on infrastructure for reinforcement learning post-training on large-scale robotic manipulation datasets. The current work is centered on making training and evaluation workflows easier to run, compare, and extend.

## Current Focus

- Organizing robotic manipulation datasets for post-training experiments.
- Building repeatable training and evaluation workflows.
- Tracking experimental assumptions, model variants, and data processing decisions.

## Personal Contribution

My role is currently infrastructure-oriented: turning research ideas into maintainable workflows that can support iteration, debugging, and comparison.
