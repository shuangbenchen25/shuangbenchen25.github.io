*[Super Brain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Brain_(game_show))*, also *The Brain*, is one of China's most well-known reality and talent show. The show's aim is to find people with exceptional brainpower. In 2018, the series was rebooted as *The Brain: Burn Your Brain*, featuring weekly puzzle-based challenges for teens and adults. It focused on mental skills over prizes.
